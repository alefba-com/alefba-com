# 🚀 ALEFBA Marketplace

Welcome to **ALEFBA** — European Online Marketplace Platform

---

## 🌍 About ALEFBA

ALEFBA is a modern multi-vendor marketplace focused on:

- Secure payments (Stripe Connect)
- Escrow-based transactions
- Seller & buyer protection
- Transparent commission system

---

## 💳 Payment System

- 0% commission (first 3 months)
- 5% commission after
- Escrow protection
- Automatic & manual payouts

---

## 🔐 Buyer Protection

- Funds released after delivery confirmation
- Auto-release after 3 days
- Dispute system with manual review

---

## 🧩 Services

- Boost & Highlight listings
- Ads system
- Pro seller accounts
- Seller verification
- Gift cards & discounts

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

- PHP
- MySQL
- Stripe
- Revolut
